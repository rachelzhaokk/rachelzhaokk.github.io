Supermodels make millions every year as they grace runways. We try to define supermodels in numbers by putting together stats of the top 50 models from 2015 to 2019.

Models.com's "top 50 models" rank models with most campaigns, covers with top magazines, fashion & cosmetic brands currently. These faces reflect the fashion industry's choices.

The tallest model among the 144 supermodels is Molly Bair (6 ft/ 183 cm). The shortest is Paloma Elsesser (5'7''/ 170 cm). The most common height is Kendall Jenner's 5'10'' or 177.8.

65 of the models is 5'10", making it also the average height. And to put it into perspective, the average height for Chinese adult women is 159.7 cm in 2014. And the nations with the tallest women in 2014 are Latvia, Netherlands, and Estonia, with an average of more than 169cm respectively.

32"24’34’ (81cm, 61cm, 87cm) was and still is the most common body measurements among models.
